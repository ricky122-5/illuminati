The Free Masons and the Illuminati

A Presentation

By Rithvik Reddygari, Edmund Garcia, Malhar Mahajan, Tomi Ayeko, and Serena Khan


Who are the Free Masons and Illuminati?

The Free Masons are an organization tracing back to the 18th century, with many different beliefs and special rituals. The Illuminati is another organization believed to make many decisions in the shadows and have many celebrities as members.

This website will go through the organizations, their origins, and their beliefs, as well as special attributes.
