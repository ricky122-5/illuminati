The Eye of Providence



The Eye

The Eye is often associated with the Illuminati in popular culture. However, it was first used in several other contexts: The Eye of Horus was an Egyptian concept of all-seeing. This is where the pyramid visuals come from. It was later adopted by Christians to represent the vigilance of God. It then became a crucial symbol of the FreeMasons. They first used the symbol in the 1700s and claim its addition on the dollar bill was intentional by FDR, a supposed member of the Masons.
